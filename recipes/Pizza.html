Pizza

Pizza is a flat, round, oven-baked bread topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various other ingredients like vegetables, meat, and spices. It's a popular dish of Italian origin, specifically from Naples, known for its diverse toppings and regional variations.>

ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Garlic Sauce
  1. Preheat oven to 365 Degrees Farenheight.
  2. Get all the ingredients together in one place.
  3. Grab the pizza dough and put the sauce onto it.
  4. Then put the cheese on top of the sauce.
  5. Put the pepperoni ontop of the Mozzarella cheese.
  6. Place in oven for 15 to 30 minutes.
  7. Pull from oven once done and put garlic sauce over crust.
  8. Enjoy!!!

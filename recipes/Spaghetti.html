Spaghetti

Spaghetti is a long, thin, cylindrical pasta made from durum wheat semolina and water. It's a staple in Italian cuisine and is known for its versatility in pairing with various sauces and toppings. The name "spaghetti" itself is derived from the Italian word "spago," meaning "string," reflecting the pasta's shape.

ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Spaghetti Sauce
  • Noodles
  • Seasonings
  1. Cook the ground beef on the stove until its brown.
  2. Cook the noodles until done
  3. Mix the noodles and beef together as well as the spaghetti sauce.
  4. Serve and Enjoy.

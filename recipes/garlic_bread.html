Garlic Bread Recipe

Description

Garlic bread is a type of bread, often made with baguette, sourdough, or ciabatta, that is flavored with garlic and butter or olive oil. It's typically toasted or baked until crispy on the outside and soft on the inside. Optional additions include herbs like oregano or chives, and cheese like mozzarella or parmesan.

Ingredients

  • butter
  • garlic
  • Parsley
  • salt
  • Loaf of Bread
  1. Gather all of the ingredients.
  2. Grab the bread and put the melted butter on it.
  3. Put the minced garlic onto the bread and ontop of the butter.
  4. Add the salt and bake for 20-30 minutes.
  5. Enjoy!!!
